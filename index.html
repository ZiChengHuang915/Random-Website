Becoming a

COMPUTER ENGINEER

A computer engineer designs computers, equipment, processors, routers, and circuit boards. They also analyze computer hardware and maintains/tests software. Another part of their job description is that they instruct programmers on how to write specific software. Many computer engineers work in research labs, manufacturing firms, or computer systems design or software publishing firms.
